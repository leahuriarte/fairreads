See all sides of the story with Fair Reads - your app for unbiased news.


Introducing Fair Reads - the app that helps you read the news with an open mind. With Fair Reads, you can easily detect bias in news articles and access other articles about the same topic with different viewpoints. Our app utilizes natural language processing to analyze news articles, and provides you with a rating based on the level of potential bias in the article. From there, you can access other articles with alternative perspectives to help you form a well-rounded opinion. With Fair Reads, you'll never have to worry about falling into an echo chamber or missing important news - try it out today and see for yourself how it can change the way you read the news.
